Mac and Chees

This recipe is so simple to make! It can be a side dish with meatloaf and stewed tomatoes or hold it's own. Enough to make 4 side dishes or 2 main dishes.

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Â¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk

STEPS

  1. Step 1: In a large pot with boiling salted water cook elbow macaroni until al dente. Drain.
  2. Step 2: In a medium saucepan, over medium heat melt butter or margarine. Whisk flour and stir vigorously.
  3. Step 3: In a large bowl mix together the drained pasta and cheese sauce mixture. Toss to coat evenly.
  4. Step 4: Pour into a greased 2 quart casserole dish. Bake in a preheated 350 degree F (175 degrees C) oven for 30 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
